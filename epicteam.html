OUR TEAMS

Our Administration Department

The Administration Department helps oversee the startups day to day operations and functions such as managing our member's workloads, helping out with product testing, final code reviews before committing to github, managing the organisation's project flow and company-public relations.

Our Coding Department

The Coding Department is the powerhouse of this organisation, here is where programmers help out with coding and error analysis before sending it for product-testing and code reviews. Programmers in this department mainly help out with writing code and helping out with issues and error fixing.

Our Research and Development Departmentt

Programmers in the R&D department are the engineers of our products as they help out with developing and engineering algorithms before being implemented in our products. Usually programmers here develope and research on concepts and algorithms for better code implementation.
