ABOUT EPICALABLE

We love coding

Introduction to our organisation

Epicalable is a brand founded and created on 2019 primarily as a youtube gaming channel. Soon the small scale startup moved into the coding world and established a base in github to post free, open-source repositories for the world to see. Our aim is to bring an end to many problems both in the computer industry and the real world through open-source, innovating and solving problems. We have worked on few projects so far with a constant hope that we can help the world in a small positive way. While we still have some ongoing and some ambitious projects coming up we will like to ask you to support our startup by simply visiting our github projects and contributing to them by starring or reporting a issue to help support us and improve our projects for others.

What we do in Epicalable?

Here at Epicalable we make programs coded in python or C++ and do web design using html and css. We usually take time developing and improving the design and code optimisation for our projects and spend time reviewing codes and also improving this website. We also learn new things as we code with the help of stackoverflow and other helpful websites. With the help of coding and free open-source projects for everyone in our society to use. Epicalable may have a few projects but we hope that it has helped people from different walks in life to help their lives feel a bit easier. Our goal to innovate and advance technology we know today might be hard but everyday new achievments and milestones are being made, so why not be a part of it here at Epicalable by contributing to our open-source projects over at github.
