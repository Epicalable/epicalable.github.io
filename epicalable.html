Introduction to our organisation

Epicalable is a brand founded and created on 2019 primarily as a youtube gaming channel. Soon the small scale organisation moved into the coding world and established a base in github to post free, open-source repositories for the world to see. Our aim is to bring an end to many problems both in the computer industry and the real world through innovating and solving problems. We have worked on few projects so far with a constant hope that we can help the world in a small positive way. While we still have some ongoing and some ambitious projects coming up we will like to ask you to support our organisation by simply visiting our github projects and contribute to them to help support us and improve our projects for others.

What we do in Epicalable?

Like we said Epicalable is an organisation that hopes to impact the world in a postive way weather big or small with the help of coding and free projects for everyone in our society to use. Epicalable may have a few projects but those have helped people from different walks in life to help their lives feel a bit easier. Our goal to innovate and advance technology we know today might be hard but everyday new achievments and milestones are being made, so why not be a part of it here at Epicalable.
